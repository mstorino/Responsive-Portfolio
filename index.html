About Me

As a student at the Northwestern University Coding Boot Camp, I am currently learning how to be a full stack developer. My interest in coding stems arose because I could not find accessible and engaging coding opportunities for my young daughters. I hope to translate the skills I learn at Northwestern into a meaningful teaching opportunity in my community.

For fun, each year I compose a blog titled Don't Call Me Oscar, in which I replicate stills from the Best Picture nominees. It has been featured in Vanity Fair, Time, People, Today, Daily Mail and several other media outlets. The 2017 Oscar photos are in the works...but first I need to finish this homework assignment.
